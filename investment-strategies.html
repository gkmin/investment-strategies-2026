Stocks & Equities

Growth-oriented ownership stakes in public companies
Core Strategy

Value Investing (Buffett-Style)

Buy stocks trading below intrinsic value and hold for 5+ years. Focus on companies with durable competitive moats, strong balance sheets, and consistent earnings.

  • Target P/E below industry average
  • Look for strong free cash flow
  • Prefer predictable, recurring revenue

Real Estate

Tangible assets generating rental income & long-term appreciation
Core Strategy

Buy & Hold Rental Properties

Acquire properties in sub-markets with steady job growth, landlord-friendly regulations, and quality-of-life anchors (hospitals, universities) for long-term cash flow.

  • Target markets: Atlanta, Dallas-Fort Worth, Raleigh
  • Cleveland: highest rent yield ratio in any US metro
  • Prioritize cash flow over appreciation in 2026
Risk
Return Potential
Active Strategy

BRRRR Method

Buy · Rehab · Rent · Refinance · Repeat — purchase below stabilized value, renovate, place tenants, then refinance at the new appraised value to recycle capital.

  • Forces equity creation through rehab
  • Refinancing returns initial capital for next deal
  • Works best in markets with value-add inventory
Risk
Return Potential
Portfolio

Diversified RE Portfolio

Spread investments across asset types to ensure stability when market conditions shift. Lower rates in 2026 are driving a real estate recovery, per Morgan Stanley.

  • Multifamily — steady demand, low vacancy
  • Build-to-Rent (BTR) — growing asset class
  • Industrial / logistics — e-commerce tailwinds
  • Select retail in high-traffic corridors

Bonds & Fixed Income

Stable income with capital preservation
Core Positioning

Intermediate-Duration Investment Grade

Charles Schwab & Vanguard recommend keeping duration in the 5–10 year range, predominantly investment-grade credit, as rates normalize in 2026.

  • Investment-grade corporates at 4–5.5% coupons
  • BBB-rated bonds offering income with low default risk
  • Intermediate Treasuries — attractive on declining rate path
Risk
Yield
Tax-Advantaged

Municipal Bonds

Muni bonds offer a compelling after-tax yield for investors in higher tax brackets. Current market offers attractive yields and stable credit quality heading into 2026.

  • Federal (and often state) tax-exempt income
  • Strong credit fundamentals post-pandemic
  • Particularly valuable in top marginal brackets
~3.5–4.5%
Tax-equivalent yield for top-bracket investors in quality munis
Inflation Hedge

TIPS — Inflation-Protected

Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities adjust principal with CPI, making them a standout opportunity when inflation uncertainty remains elevated.

  • Principal rises with CPI automatically
  • Real yield remains positive in 2026
  • Ideal for retirement portfolios & conservative allocators

Tangible stores of value and inflation hedges
Bullish Outlook

Gold — Institutional Bull Case

J.P. Morgan forecasts gold reaching $5,000/oz by year-end 2026. Yardeni Research targets $6,000. Central bank demand is averaging 585 tonnes per quarter, sustaining the bull run.

  • Geopolitical uncertainty driving safe-haven demand
  • Emerging market central banks buying aggressively
  • Low correlation to equities (0.1–0.3 vs S&P 500)

Access Methods

How to Invest in Gold

Investors must decide between direct physical exposure and financial instruments. Each has distinct risk/cost trade-offs.

  • Physical — coins, bars, bullion (storage & insurance costs)
  • Gold ETFs — GLD, IAU track spot price without storage
  • Mining stocks — GDX, GDXJ offer operating leverage to gold
  • Gold IRA — tax-advantaged retirement exposure
